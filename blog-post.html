Entity SEO: Build Topics, Not Keywords

Published Sep 3, 2025 ‚Ä¢ 6 min read

Search is shifting to entities and relationships. Instead of isolated keywords, focus your site on topics with structured internal links and schema.

Why entities?

  • They mirror how knowledge is stored.
  • Enable rich results with schema.org.
  • Support AI answer engines.

How to start

  1. Define your topical map and main entities.
  2. Create pillar pages and supporting clusters.
  3. Add Organization/Person/Article schema.
